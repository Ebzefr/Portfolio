About me.

I’m a final-year Computer Science student at the University of Chester and the founder of Clouddey and WebSecura, two ventures focused on cloud automation and web security. I’m passionate about building practical tech solutions that make life easier, and over the past few years, I’ve developed full-stack applications ranging from secure restaurant apps to web vulnerability scanners and data dashboards.

I’m currently seeking employment opportunities, open to relocation, and eager to bring curiosity, grit, and technical problem-solving to the next team I join.
